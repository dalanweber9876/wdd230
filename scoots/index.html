Vacations Should Be Stress Free

At Scoots, we believe that vacations are meant for relaxation, exploration, and creating unforgettable memories. That's why we've designed our rental services to make your Cozumel getaway as stress-free as possible. Here are some things that we offer.

Hassle-Free Rentals

Enjoy a seamless rental process with Scoots. Whether you're a local looking for a quick ride or a tourist planning ahead, we've got you covered. Our user-friendly reservation system allows you to secure your preferred vehicle with just a $50 security deposit, giving you peace of mind.

Variety of Options

Choose from our diverse fleet of Honda Motor Scooters, Side-by-Side ATVs, and Jeeps. Whether you're riding solo or with a group, we have the perfect vehicle for your island adventure. From the nimble Honda Metropolitan to the rugged Jeep Wrangler, our rentals cater to every preference.

Transparent Pricing

We believe in transparency, and that extends to our pricing. Check out our competitive rates for half-day and full-day rentals. Plus, with unlimited mileage and included liability insurance, you can focus on the journey, not the details.

Delivery Convenience

For added convenience, we offer free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for rentals of two days or more. No need to worry about transportation – we'll bring your chosen vehicle to you.

Easy Payment Options

Making a reservation is simple, and so is payment. Choose from various payment methods, including Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.

Explore Stress-Free

Vacations are a time to explore, discover, and smile. Let Scoots be your trusted companion on the roads of Cozumel. With our reliable rentals and hassle-free services, you can focus on the beauty of the island and create lasting memories. Your stress-free vacation starts with Scoots. Reserve your ride today!
