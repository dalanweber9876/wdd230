Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Rexburg Chamber of Commerce is designed to provide access to resources and relationships that help businesses in the area grow. It provides a platform in which businesses can market their products to help reach the people of the city. The Rexburg Chamber of Commerce updates the city on any events that are taking place.

Scenarios

  • I'm visiting Rexburg, what are the hotels, restaurants, entertainment, etc in the area?
  • What events are coming up so that I can support local business?
  • I have a new business and want to get it out there for everyone to see. How do I join the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce?

Color Schema

I will use Cerulean on the header/navigation bar and the footer. I will use Verdigris as a background color for my section headers. I will use Gunmetal if I feel like the Chamber website could use more/different colors.

Typography

I plan on using "Playfair" font for all of the text in the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce website (Currently in use).
