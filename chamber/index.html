Rexburg city council gathered on Wednesday for bi-monthly meeting

The Rexburg City Council held its bi-monthly meeting, addressing various community matters after recent municipal elections. The mayor announced a $2 million grant for street improvements, and the Rexburg Police Department recognized officers for certifications. Committee reports covered topics like parking around Porter Park, upcoming community events, and city structure updates. The finance report included the High Five Grant for a community kitchen in a food truck. Public Works discussed airport relocation, and Planning and Zoning proposed a city sign upgrade amendment. The council approved several proposals, and a public hearing for rate and fee changes is scheduled for December 6.
